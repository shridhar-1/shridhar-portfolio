About Honnavar

Welcome to Honnavar, a coastal gem where the river Sharavathi meets the Arabian Sea. From the hanging bridge to the golden sands of Eco Beach, experience nature at its purest.

Top Attractions

  • Eco Beach (Kasarkod): A pristine "Blue Flag" certified beach.
  • Sharavathi Backwaters: Enjoy boating in the calm waters.
  • Apsarakonda Falls: The "Pond of Angels" with a sunset view park.
  • Hanging Bridge: A perfect spot for evening photography.
  • Karikala parameshwari devi temple: A hidden spot ,Honnavar Heaven
